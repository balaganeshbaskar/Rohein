Who We Are

Located in the heart of Hosur, we are pioneers in plant tissue culture with over 15 years of expertise.

Our mission is to deliver sustainable, high-quality plant propagation solutions, empowering farmers, horticulturists, and industries to achieve healthier yields and greener outcomes. Combining cutting-edge technology with a passion for innovation, we specialize in producing disease-free, genetically uniform plants that meet the highest standards of quality and sustainability. At the core of our work is a commitment to driving agricultural advancement and fostering a more sustainable future.
